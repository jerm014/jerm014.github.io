SafeOfMind.com

AI-Powered Mental Health Crisis Support Platform

SafeOfMind.com is a comprehensive web platform designed to provide immediate, accessible mental health support during crisis situations. Built with a user-centered approach, the platform combines AI-powered guidance with curated local and national mental health resources to create a bridge between individuals in crisis and professional help.

  • AI-Powered Crisis Support
    Integrated Anthropic's Claude Haiku foundation model to provide empathetic, real-time mental health guidance through natural language processing.
  • Comprehensive Resource Database
    Curated and maintains a database of local, state, and national mental health resources including crisis hotlines, counseling services, and emergency contacts.
  • Scalable Cloud Architecture
    Built on AWS serverless infrastructure using Lambda and Bedrock, achieving 99.9999% uptime with automatic scaling capabilities to handle traffic spikes during crisis periods, reducing response times to under 200ms.
